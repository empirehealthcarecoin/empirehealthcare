Maintenance & Repairs

Downtime is not an option in healthcare. Our dedicated service team ensures your equipment operates at peak efficiency throughout its lifecycle. With a focus on preventive care and rapid response, we keep your diagnostic services running smoothly.

Service Contracts

  • AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract): Scheduled preventive maintenance visits to clean, calibrate, and inspect equipment.
  • CMC (Comprehensive Maintenance Contract): All-inclusive coverage including spare parts and labor for total peace of mind.
  • On-Call Service: Flexible breakdown support for clients without contracts.

Spare Parts & Repairs

  • Genuine Spares: We stock authentic Mindray parts (probes, boards, power supplies) for longevity.
  • Probe Repair: Specialized repair services for ultrasound probes (lens replacement, cable repair).
  • Quick Turnaround: Local inventory of critical parts to minimize waiting time.

Preventive Maintenance

  • Software Updates: Installing the latest firmware patches for security and performance.
  • Deep Cleaning: Internal dust removal to prevent overheating and circuit failure.
  • Electrical Safety: Leakage current testing to ensure patient and operator safety.
