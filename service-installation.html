Installation & Setup

A great product is only as good as its installation. Our certified engineering team ensures that your biomedical equipment is unboxed, assembled, and calibrated to factory standards, ensuring patient safety and regulatory compliance from day one.

Site Planning

  • Pre-Install Inspection: Assessing electrical grounding, space requirements, and environmental conditions.
  • Layout Design: Consulting on ergonomic placement of equipment for optimal workflow.
  • Safety Check: Verifying UPS and voltage stabilizers to protect sensitive electronics.

Professional Installation

  • Assembly: Expert assembly of carts, arms, and peripheral devices.
  • Calibration: Fine-tuning software settings and probe calibration for peak performance.
  • Networking: Connecting equipment to PACS, DICOM servers, or hospital networks (HIS/RIS).

Handover

  • Performance Verification: Running phantom tests to ensure image/result accuracy.
  • Documentation: Providing installation reports and warranty activation certificates.
  • Initial Briefing: Basic operational walkthrough for immediate use.
