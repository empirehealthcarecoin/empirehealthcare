Mindray DP-50 Expert

Diagnostic Confidence with Exceptional Mobility

The DP-50 Expert is a high-performance, portable black & white ultrasound system designed to meet the evolving needs of general practice. Engineered for deep penetration and fast image acquisition, it combines a smart, compact shape with advanced imaging technologies like PW Doppler and Phase Shift Harmonic Imaging.

Key Highlights

  • Crystal Clear Imaging: Powered by iBeam™, iClear™, and PSHI™ technologies.
  • Advanced Workflow: iTouch™ Auto Optimization and iStation™ patient management.
  • Mobility: Lightweight design (8.1kg) with 1.5hr battery life.
  • Versatility: Supports OB/GYN, MSK, Urology, and Orthopedics.

Advanced Imaging Technologies

  • PSHI™ (Phase Shift Harmonic Imaging): Purified Harmonic Imaging for better contrast resolution providing clearer images with excellent resolution and less noise.
  • iBeam™: Permits use of multiple scanned angles to form a single image, resulting in enhanced contrast resolution and improved visualization.
  • iClear™: Adaptive Speckle Suppression Imaging for sharper edges and smooth uniform tissues.

Intelligent Workflow

  • iTouch™: Instant auto image optimization in B mode with the click of a single key.
  • iZoom™: Gain instant full-screen view.
  • iStation™: Mindray’s unique Patient Information Management System allows you to integrate, review, archive, and retrieve patient data effectively.
Display 15-inch LCD with 60-degree tilt angle
Weight ~8.1 kg (highly portable)

Imaging Modes B, 2B, 4B, B+M, M, PW Doppler (Optional/Model dependent)

Clinical Versatility

The DP-50 Expert is equipped with a wide range of transducers to support diverse clinical requirements:

  • Abdomen: Deep penetration for difficult-to-scan patients.
  • Obstetrics & Gynecology: Detailed fetal visualization.
  • Urology: Precision imaging for kidneys, bladder, and prostate.
  • Musculoskeletal (MSK): High-frequency linear probes for superficial structures.
  • Small Parts: Thyroid, breast, and testicular imaging.
