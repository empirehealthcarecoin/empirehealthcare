Mindray DC-60 X-Insight

Technology You Can Touch. Insight You Can Trust.

The DC-60 with X-Insight is a premium ultrasound system that focuses on what matters most to clinicians: precision imaging and immediate workflow. Powered by the new X-Engine, it integrates CPU and GPU processing for significantly faster imaging and clarity, making it the optimal choice for shared services.

Key Highlights

  • X-Engine: Integrated GPU and CPU architecture for multi-core parallel processing.
  • Single Crystal Transducers: Deeper penetration and higher resolution for difficult patients.
  • Immersive Experience: 21.5-inch Full HD Monitor with dual-wing articulating arm.
  • Touch Operation: 13.3-inch ultra-slim touch screen with gesture control.
  • Smart Planes CNS: Fully automated detection of fetal CNS planes.

eXceptional Intelligence

  • ComboWave Transducers: Utilizes a new type of piezoelectric material to optimize the acoustic spectrum.
  • Smart Face: Fast and intelligent optimization for fetal face with one-touch operation, removing occlusions.
  • Smart FLC: Automatically detects the number and calculates the volume of follicles from a 3D volume image.
  • iLive: Realistic rendering mode integrating traditional ray casting with new virtual lighting.

eXceeding Efficiency

  • X-Engine: 3X to 4X faster signal processing compared to traditional systems.
  • MedTouch: Remote control of the ultrasound system via Android/iOS devices.
  • iWorks™: Standardized workflow protocol to reduce keystrokes and exam time by up to 50%.

Shared Service Excellence

The DC-60 X-Insight is built to handle high-volume, diverse caseloads:

  • Women's Health: Advanced 3D/4D capabilities, Smart Planes CNS, and Smart Face for detailed fetal assessment.
  • Radiology: Superior penetration with Single Crystal probes for abdominal and small parts imaging.
  • Cardiology: Complete cardiac assessment tools including TDI, Tissue Tracking, and Auto EF.
  • Vascular: High-sensitivity color doppler and automated IMT measurements.
