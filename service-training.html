Training & Support

Technology advances rapidly, and we ensure your team keeps up. Our Application Specialists provide in-depth training to help your doctors and technicians utilize the full potential of your Mindray equipment, optimizing patient care.

Application Training

  • On-Site Training: Personalized sessions for doctors to master features like 4D imaging, Elastography, and Auto-Measurements.
  • Technician Workshops: Teaching lab staff best practices for sample handling and analyzer maintenance.
  • Refresher Courses: Regular updates when new software features or upgrades are released.

Technical Support

  • 24/7 Helpline: Immediate assistance for critical breakdowns or software errors.
  • Remote Diagnostics: Solving minor software issues remotely to save time.
  • User Manuals: Providing simplified quick-reference guides for daily operations.

Post-Sales Care

  • Dedicated Specialist: A specific application specialist assigned to your account.
  • Feedback Loop: Regular check-ins to ensure the equipment meets your clinical expectations.
