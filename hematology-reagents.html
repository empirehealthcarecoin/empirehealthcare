Mindray Hematology Reagents

Original System Reagents for Peak Performance

Using original Mindray reagents is crucial for ensuring the reliability and accuracy of your hematology analyzer results. Designed and manufactured specifically for the BC-Series analyzers, these reagents guarantee optimal system performance, result validity, and long-term instrument stability.

Key Highlights

  • System Match: Formulation strictly matched to Mindray analyzer fluidics.
  • High Stability: Long shelf life and stability after opening.
  • Cyanide-Free: Environmentally friendly reagents for Hemoglobin testing.
  • Quality Assurance: Manufactured under strict ISO quality control standards.

Comprehensive Reagent Portfolio

  • Diluent: Maintains cell shape and volume for accurate counting.
  • Lyse: Breaks down red blood cells to liberate hemoglobin and differentiate white blood cells.
  • Cleaner: Highly effective enzymatic cleaner to prevent protein buildup and blockages.
  • Probe Cleanser: Specialized maintenance solution for deep cleaning of the sample probe.

Why Choose Original?
