Mindray CLIA Controls

Calibrators & Controls for CL-Series

Mindray offers a complete menu of calibrators and quality controls for Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). Manufactured with high standards to ensure traceability and stability, these reagents are essential for obtaining accurate and reliable results on all Mindray CL-Series analyzers.

Key Highlights

  • Traceability: Calibrators traceable to international standards (WHO, IFCC).
  • Commutability: High commutability with patient samples for confident quality assurance.
  • Wide Menu: Covers Thyroid, Fertility, Tumor Markers, Cardiac, and more.
  • Ready-to-Use: Liquid stable formulations for convenience and reduced error.

Comprehensive Test Categories

  • Thyroid: T3, T4, FT3, FT4, TSH, TG, Anti-TG, Anti-TPO
  • Fertility: FSH, LH, PRL, Estradiol, Progesterone, Testosterone, HCG
  • Tumor Markers: CEA, AFP, CA125, CA15-3, CA19-9, PSA, fPSA
  • Cardiac: Troponin I, CK-MB mass, Myoglobin, BNP/NT-proBNP
  • Infectious Disease: HBsAg, Anti-HBs, HBeAg, Anti-HBe, Anti-HBc, HIV, Syphilis
  • Metabolism: Insulin, C-Peptide, Cortisol, ACTH, 25-OH Vitamin D
  • Anemia: Ferritin, Vitamin B12, Folate
  • Inflammation: PCT, IL-6

Reliability You Can Trust

  • International Standards: Calibrators are anchored to reference materials from WHO and other international bodies to ensure accuracy.
  • Stability: Excellent open-vial and long-term stability reduces waste and frequency of calibration.
  • Multi-Level Controls: Available in 2 or 3 levels to monitor the analytical range effectively.
