Mindray DC-40 Crystal

Innovative. Intelligent. Inspirational.

The DC-40 Crystal Series brings a new level of high-performance capability to primary care. Utilizing Mindray's advanced ComboWave transducers and crystal-clear imaging technologies, it offers exceptional diagnostic confidence. With a gesture-sensitive touch screen and intelligent OB tools, the DC-40 Crystal is designed for efficiency.

Key Highlights

  • Crystal Clarity: Equipped with ComboWave transducers for enhanced sensitivity.
  • Immersive Display: 21.5-inch Full HD Monitor & 13.3-inch Ultra-slim Touch Screen.
  • Smart Intelligence: Features Smart OB, Smart NT, and Smart Plane CNS.
  • Realistic 4D: iLive technology for photorealistic fetal views.
  • Natural Touch Elastography: Superior stiffness assessment with high reproducibility.

Advanced Imaging & Transducers

  • ComboWave Transducers: New piezoelectric composite material significantly optimizes the acoustic spectrum and reduces acoustic impedance.
  • iClear™: Speckle-reduction imaging technology for reduced noise and enhanced contrast resolution.
  • PSH™ (Phase Shift Harmonic): Purified harmonic imaging for clearer contrast and higher resolution.

Intelligent Workflow Tools

  • iLive: Integrates traditional ray casting algorithm with a new virtual lighting modality for realistic visual effects.
  • Smart Plane CNS: Automated detection of the most significant planes and measurements of fetal CNS.
  • Smart Face: Fast and intelligent optimization for fetal face with one-touch operation.
  • iTouch™: Instant auto image optimization in B, Color, and PW modes.

Clinical Versatility

The DC-40 Crystal provides a comprehensive solution for diverse clinical scenarios:

  • OB/GYN: Exceptional 3D/4D imaging with iLive, Smart Face, and accurate biometry tools.
  • General Imaging: High-resolution imaging for Abdomen, Small Parts, and Thyroid.
  • Cardiology: Complete cardiac package including TDI, Auto EF, and Free Xros M.
  • Vascular: Auto IMT for precise carotid intima-media thickness measurement.
  • Urology: Detailed imaging of kidneys, bladder, and prostate.
