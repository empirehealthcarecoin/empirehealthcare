Customized Healthcare Solutions

Every healthcare facility is unique. Whether you are launching a new diagnostic center or upgrading an existing hospital department, Empire Healthcare offers tailored solutions that align with your clinical goals, budget, and future growth plans.

Start-Up Consultancy

  • Turnkey Projects: End-to-end support for setting up new ultrasound and pathology labs.
  • Budget Planning: Selecting the right mix of equipment (Entry-level vs. High-end) to maximize ROI.
  • Regulatory Guidance: Assisting with compliance requirements for equipment installation (PNDT etc.).

Workflow Optimization

  • Digital Integration: Setting up DICOM/PACS solutions for paperless workflow.
  • Department Layout: Designing efficient scanning rooms and lab areas to reduce staff fatigue.
  • Upgrade Paths: Buy-back programs and exchange offers for upgrading old equipment.

Specialty Packages

  • Women's Health Package: Curated set of Ultrasound (Voluson/Mindray) + Fetal Monitors.
  • Cardiac Package: Echo machines + TMT + Holter setup.
  • Rural Health Kit: Rugged, portable, battery-operated equipment for remote camps.
